Role of genetics on mental illnesses.

Matia Çela
2024·02·23

The nature of mental illnesses is complex and influenced by a combination of genetic, enviromental, and other factors. Genetic predisposition plays a key role in their development. Some studies have identified specific genetic variations associated with increased risk of developing mental illnesses as depression, generalized anxiety disorder, schizophrenia, etc

However, its important to understand that genetic predisposition alone is not the only factor in the onset of these illnesses. Environmental factors such as stress, traumatic events , changes in family structure, and other environmental factors also have significant impact on their development.
