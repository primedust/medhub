Androgen steroids are a class of hormones that include testosterone. They play a crucial role in the development of male characteristics, such as facial hair, deepening of the voice, and muscle growth. In medicine, androgens may be prescribed to treat conditions like hypogonadism. However, non-medical use of androgen steroids for performance enhancement is a form of substance abuse and can have serious health consequences.

Pros of androgen steroids when used medically include treating conditions like hypogonadism, muscle-wasting diseases, and delayed puberty. They can enhance muscle growth, strength, and overall physical performance.

Cons include potential side effects such as liver damage, cardiovascular issues, hormonal imbalances, mood swings, and the risk of addiction. Non-medical use for performance enhancement can lead to serious health risks and is often associated with ethical concerns and legal consequences. It's crucial to use androgen steroids under medical supervision and only when medically necessary.
